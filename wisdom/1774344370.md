# Fascinating Facts about Octopi

Did you know that octopi have three hearts? Two pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body. Even more wild: when an octopus swims, the heart pumping to the body actually stops, which is why they prefer crawling!

Also, octopi can taste with their arms. Each sucker contains millions of taste receptors, so they're constantly sampling their environment. Imagine if your hands could taste everything you touched!
