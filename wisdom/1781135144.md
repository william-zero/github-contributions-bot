# Strange But True Facts

## The Slowest Animal Race Nobody Won

The three-toed sloth moves so slowly that algae actually grows on its fur — turning it green and giving it natural camouflage in the rainforest canopy. This is evolution being absolutely unhinged: the sloth is too slow to clean itself, so it outsourced camouflage to a plant.

## Sound Has a Shape

When you play a specific sound frequency over a plate covered in fine sand (a technique called cymatics), the sand arranges itself into geometric patterns — different notes create completely different shapes, from simple rings to intricate snowflakes. Music is literally drawing pictures.
