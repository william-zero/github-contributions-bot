# Interesting Facts

## Mantis Shrimp Eyes
Mantis shrimp have 16 color receptors in their eyes (humans have 3). They can see ultraviolet, visible, and polarized light, making them one of the most complex visual systems in the animal kingdom.

## The Probability of Your Existence
The odds of you existing are about 1 in 400 trillion. Your parents had to meet at exactly the right time, and the same goes for their parents, and so on.
