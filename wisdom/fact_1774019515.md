# Nature Facts

Did you know that axolotls can regenerate lost limbs, eyes, and even parts of their heart and brain? These aquatic salamanders can regrow damaged tissue perfectly, making them invaluable for medical research.

The mantis shrimp has the most complex eyes in the animal kingdom, with 16 color receptors compared to humans' measly 3. They can see ultraviolet, visible, and polarized light all at once!
