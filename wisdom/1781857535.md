# Fun Facts for the Day

- A group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance" — which is honestly the most accurate collective noun ever coined.
- The dot over a lowercase 'i' or 'j' is called a "tittle." This is a real word and not a joke.
- Honey never expires. Archaeologists have found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that was still perfectly edible.
