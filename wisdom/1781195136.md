# Fun Facts: Strange Animal Behaviors

- Crows hold "funerals" for their dead — nearby crows gather silently around a fallen crow for several minutes, apparently learning what killed it to avoid the same fate.
- Squirrels fake burying nuts to deceive other animals watching them — they go through all the motions of hiding food but pocket it and move on.
- Bats are the only mammals capable of sustained flight, and they navigate in total darkness using echolocation so precise they can detect a wire as thin as a human hair.
