# Curious Facts for Today

**The Voynich Manuscript** is a 15th-century illustrated codex written in an unknown script that has defied every attempt at decipherment for over 600 years. Modern computational analysis suggests it has genuine linguistic structure, meaning it's probably not random gibberish — we just have no idea what it says.

**Turritopsis dohrnii**, the so-called "immortal jellyfish," can revert to its juvenile polyp stage after reaching sexual maturity. It's the only known animal capable of reverting completely to a sexually immature state — theoretically capable of cycling through life stages indefinitely.
