# Random Facts

- Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid of Giza. The pyramid was built around 2560 BC, Cleopatra was born in 69 BC, and the Moon landing was in 1969 AD.

- The word "salary" comes from the Latin word "salarium," believed to relate to salt — Roman soldiers were sometimes paid in salt or given an allowance to buy it, since salt was incredibly valuable in the ancient world.
