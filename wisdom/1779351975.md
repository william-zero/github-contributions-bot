# Today's Wonderfully Useless Facts

- Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid of Giza. The pyramids were built around 2560 BCE, Cleopatra lived around 69–30 BCE, and the Moon landing was 1969 CE. History is wild.

- The total weight of all ants on Earth is roughly equal to the total weight of all humans. There are an estimated 20 quadrillion ants alive right now, and they are absolutely not planning anything.
