# The Bioluminescent Communication of Fireflies

Fireflies produce light through a chemical reaction called bioluminescence. Each species has a unique flashing pattern—essentially their own morse code love language. Males flash from the ground, females flash back from vegetation in carefully timed responses. It's nature's most romantic light show and the world's first fiber optics network!

# Why Dolphins Sleep with One Eye Open

Dolphins can't afford to sleep deeply like humans. They have evolved unihemispheric sleep—literally sleeping with only half their brain at a time. One hemisphere rests while the other stays alert for predators, air, and obstacles. This means they never truly close both eyes, maintaining perfect vigilance while catching Z's.
