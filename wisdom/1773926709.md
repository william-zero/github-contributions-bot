# Quantum Quirks

Did you know that Schrödinger's cat was actually a thought experiment meant to show how absurd it would be to apply quantum mechanics to everyday objects? Erwin Schrödinger himself was skeptical of the many-worlds interpretation!

Quantum entanglement doesn't allow faster-than-light communication, but it does let two particles be mysteriously correlated. Einstein famously called this "spooky action at a distance" and he was right to be spooked!
