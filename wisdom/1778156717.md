# Daily Facts

- Tardigrades (water bears) can survive in the vacuum of space, withstand radiation doses 1000x lethal to humans, and even endure temperatures from -272°C to 150°C — making them Earth's most indestructible animals.
- The human brain generates about 70,000 thoughts per day, and roughly 80% of them are negative — a cognitive bias called "negativity bias" that evolved as a survival mechanism.
