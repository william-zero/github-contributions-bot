# Weekend Curiosities

- A group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance" — which is arguably the best collective noun in existence.
- Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that was still perfectly edible.
- The Eiffel Tower grows about 15 cm taller in summer because heat causes the iron to expand.
- Crows can recognize and remember human faces, and they hold grudges — they've been observed teaching their offspring to distrust specific people.
