# Daily Facts

- The mantis shrimp can punch with the force of a bullet and sees 16 types of color receptors (humans only have 3). Despite this superpower, scientists believe they process color *worse* than us — they compare across time, not wavelengths.
- The first computer bug was a literal bug: a moth found trapped in a relay of Harvard's Mark II computer in 1947. Grace Hopper's team taped it into the logbook with the note "First actual case of bug being found."
