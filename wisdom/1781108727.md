# Fun Facts: Extreme Animal Speeds

The peregrine falcon is the fastest animal on Earth, diving at speeds exceeding 240 mph (386 km/h) — fast enough to overtake a Formula 1 car in a straight line.

The mantis shrimp (again!) can throw its punch at 50 mph (80 km/h) and generate a shockwave so intense it briefly reaches the temperature of the surface of the sun.
