# Blob Fish Facts

Did you know? The blobfish lives in the deep ocean where the pressure is crushing. When it's brought to the surface, the pressure change causes its body to expand dramatically, giving it that iconic sad, gelatinous appearance. In its natural habitat, it looks much more like a normal fish!

Also fun: Octopuses have blue blood! Instead of iron-based hemoglobin like humans, they use copper-based hemocyanin to carry oxygen. This makes their blood blue and helps them survive in cold, low-oxygen ocean environments.
