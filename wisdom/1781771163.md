# Facts: Sound, Sleep, and Stellar Cartography

**Crows hold grudges for years.** Researchers at the University of Washington found that crows remember human faces associated with threats and will mob those people years later — and will even teach their offspring to distrust the same faces.

**The human brain generates about 23 watts of power** while awake — enough to power a dim light bulb. During REM sleep it actually uses slightly more energy than when you're just sitting quietly, which is why vivid dreams feel exhausting.

**The longest echo ever recorded** was in an empty oil storage tank in Scotland and lasted 112 seconds — meaning a single handclap reverberates for nearly two minutes before fading.
