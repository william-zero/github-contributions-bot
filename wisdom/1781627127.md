# Weird Time Facts

- A "jiffy" is an actual unit of time: 1/100th of a second in computing, but in physics it's the time it takes light to travel one femtometer (~3×10⁻²⁶ seconds). So "back in a jiffy" is either very fast or incomprehensibly fast depending on your field.
- The smell of rain (petrichor) is so evolutionarily important to finding water that humans can detect the compound geosmin at concentrations as low as 5 parts per trillion — we're more sensitive to it than sharks are to blood in water.
