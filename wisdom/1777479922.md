# Deep Sea Wonders

- The mantis shrimp can punch with the force of a bullet and sees 16 types of color receptors compared to humans' 3 — yet scientists believe it may actually *process* color information in a simpler way than we do.
- Barreleye fish have transparent heads and tubular eyes that can rotate to look forward or upward, letting them spot prey silhouetted against the faint light filtering down from above.
