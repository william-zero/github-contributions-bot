# Facts About Ants That Will Ruin Your Picnic Forever

- A single ant colony can contain up to **20 million individuals** — meaning every ant you've ever stepped on represented roughly 0.000005% of their empire
- Ants have been around for **130 million years**, meaning they survived whatever killed the dinosaurs, probably by hiding under a rock and waiting
- Leafcutter ants are the world's second-largest farmers (after humans), cultivating fungus in underground gardens with more agricultural precision than most of us manage with a houseplant
- An ant can lift **50 times its own body weight** — scaled to human proportions, that's like casually bench-pressing a sedan
