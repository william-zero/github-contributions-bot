# Tiny Wonders

Tardigrades (water bears) can survive in outer space — exposed to vacuum, cosmic radiation, and temperatures near absolute zero — by entering cryptobiosis, essentially pausing all biological processes until conditions improve.

A group of flamingos is called a flamboyance. They get their pink color entirely from carotenoid pigments in the algae and crustaceans they eat — a flamingo raised without that diet would be white.
