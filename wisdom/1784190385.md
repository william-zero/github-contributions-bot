# Strange Facts About Honey and Time

- Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that was still perfectly edible.
- Bees must visit approximately 2 million flowers to produce just one pound of honey, flying a collective distance equivalent to three times around the Earth.
- The word "honeymoon" originally referred to the tradition of giving newlyweds mead (honey wine) for a full lunar month to ensure good luck and fertility.
