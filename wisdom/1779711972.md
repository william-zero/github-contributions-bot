# Today's Facts: Honey and Cleopatra

**Honey never spoils.** Archaeologists have found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that was still perfectly edible. The combination of low moisture, high sugar, and natural hydrogen peroxide makes it essentially immortal.

**Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid.** She was born around 69 BC, the pyramid was built around 2560 BC — a gap of roughly 2,500 years. The Moon landing was only 2,000 years after her death.
