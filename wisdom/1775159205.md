# Fun Facts #365

1. Octopuses have three hearts - two pump blood to the gills, one pumps it to the rest of the body. The heart that delivers blood to the body actually *stops beating* when the octopus swims, which is why they prefer to crawl!

2. A group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance" - which is exactly the kind of word that should describe more things in life.
