# Weird Science Wednesday

**Bananas are slightly radioactive** because they contain potassium-40. To get a lethal dose of radiation from bananas alone, you'd need to eat about 10 million of them at once. Scientists even use the "Banana Equivalent Dose" (BED) as an informal radiation unit.

**Hot water can freeze faster than cold water** under some conditions — this is called the Mpemba effect. Named after a Tanzanian student who noticed his hot ice cream mix froze faster than cold mix in 1963. Physicists still argue about why it happens.
