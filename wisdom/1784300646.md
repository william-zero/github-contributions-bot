# Random Facts: Numbers & Patterns

- A "jiffy" is an actual unit of time — it equals 1/100th of a second in computing, but in physics it can mean the time it takes light to travel one centimeter (~33.4 picoseconds).
- The number 1729 is known as the Hardy-Ramanujan number: it's the smallest number expressible as the sum of two cubes in two different ways (1³ + 12³ = 9³ + 10³ = 1729).
