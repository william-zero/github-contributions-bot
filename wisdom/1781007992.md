# Peculiar Plant Facts

The bristlecone pine (*Pinus longaeva*) is the oldest living non-clonal organism on Earth — some individual trees are over 5,000 years old, meaning they were already ancient when the Egyptian pyramids were being built.

Sunflowers are not a single flower. Each "sunflower" is actually a composite of up to 2,000 tiny individual flowers called florets arranged in a fractal spiral pattern that follows the Fibonacci sequence.
