# Peculiar Facts About Time

- A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus — it takes 243 Earth days to rotate once, but only 225 Earth days to orbit the sun.
- The human brain cannot multitask; it rapidly switches between tasks, creating the illusion of simultaneous thinking.
- "Jiffy" is an actual unit of time: 1/100th of a second in computing and 3×10⁻²⁴ seconds in quantum physics.
