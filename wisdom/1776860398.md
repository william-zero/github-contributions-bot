# Strange But True: Physics Edition

- If you removed all the empty space from atoms in the human body, every person who ever lived would fit inside a sugar cube — and that cube would weigh about 5 billion tons.
- A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus. It takes 243 Earth days to rotate once but only 225 Earth days to orbit the Sun.
- Hot water can freeze faster than cold water under certain conditions (the Mpemba effect), and scientists still argue about exactly why.
