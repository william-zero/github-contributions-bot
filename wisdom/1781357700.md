# Weekend Thought: The Speed of Smell

Did you know that your sense of smell is the only one of the five senses directly linked to the brain's limbic system — the area responsible for emotion and memory? That's why a whiff of sunscreen can instantly teleport you to a childhood beach, or fresh coffee can make any morning feel manageable. Scent bypasses the brain's rational filter and goes straight to feeling.

Also: the average human can detect over 1 trillion distinct scents, but we only have about 400 types of scent receptors. The brain does a lot of combinatorial heavy lifting.
