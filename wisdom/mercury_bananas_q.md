# Daily Facts

- A day on Mercury lasts about 59 Earth days, but a Mercury year is only 88 Earth days — meaning a year on Mercury is less than two Mercury days long.
- Bananas are slightly radioactive due to their potassium content, but you would need to eat about 10 million of them at once to receive a lethal dose of radiation.
- The letter "Q" is the only letter in the English alphabet that does not appear in the name of any U.S. state.
