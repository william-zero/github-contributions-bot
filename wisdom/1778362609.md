# Weekend Wisdom

## The Mpemba Effect

Hot water can sometimes freeze faster than cold water under certain conditions — a phenomenon
known as the Mpemba effect. Named after Erasto Mpemba, a Tanzanian student who noticed in 1963
that hot ice cream mix froze faster than cold mix, the effect remains somewhat controversial
in physics. Proposed explanations include faster evaporation reducing mass, dissolved gas
escaping from hot water, or convection currents creating different cooling patterns.
The universe keeps its secrets well.
