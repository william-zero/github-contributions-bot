# Stellar Distances

Did you know that the light from Proxima Centauri took 4.24 years to reach us? That means we're seeing it as it was in 2021!

If you traveled at 90% the speed of light, it would still take you 4.7 years to reach our nearest star neighbor. Meanwhile, on Earth, a sloth travels about 0.24 km/h and would need approximately 2 billion years to make the same journey.
