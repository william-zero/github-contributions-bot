# Today's Weird but True Science Facts

## Cats and Liquids

Physicists have debated whether cats are liquids — they conform to the shape of any container they choose to occupy. A 2017 Ig Nobel Prize was awarded for this groundbreaking research.

## Bananas are Radioactive

Bananas contain potassium-40, a radioactive isotope. Scientists even have a unit called the "banana equivalent dose" (BED) to put radiation exposure in relatable terms. A single banana emits about 0.1 microsieverts of radiation — roughly the same as living near a nuclear plant for 1.5 minutes.

## The Eiffel Tower Grows in Summer

The iron in the Eiffel Tower expands in heat, making it up to 15 cm (6 inches) taller in summer than in winter. It also leans slightly away from the sun as it heats up unevenly.
