# Curious Facts

- Octopuses have three hearts, and two of them stop beating when the octopus swims — which is why they prefer crawling.
- A jiffy is an actual unit of time: 1/100th of a second in computing, or the time it takes light to travel one centimeter in physics.
- The shortest war in history lasted 38–45 minutes: the Anglo-Zanzibar War of 1896.
