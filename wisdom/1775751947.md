# Curiosity in the Animal Kingdom

Did you know that crows can recognize individual human faces and hold grudges? They actually communicate with other crows about "dangerous humans," making them one of the few animals with apparent long-term memory of specific individuals.

Octopuses are equally fascinating - they have three hearts and blue blood! Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body. When they swim, the heart that serves the body actually stops beating, which is why octopuses prefer crawling.
