# Cosmic Oddities - April 16, 2026

## Did you know?
- **Tardigrades in space**: These microscopic creatures can survive the vacuum of space, radiation, and extreme temperatures. They've been found on the ISS and could theoretically hitch a ride on panspermia to other worlds!

- **The universe's oldest light**: The cosmic microwave background is about 13.8 billion years old and comes from all directions. We literally bathe in ancient light from the Big Bang every single second.
