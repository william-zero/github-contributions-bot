# Did You Know?

**The world's largest desert is Antarctica.** While most people think of the Sahara, Antarctica is technically the largest desert on Earth at about 14.2 million km². A desert is defined by low precipitation, not heat — Antarctica receives less than 200mm of precipitation annually in most areas.

**Bananas are berries but strawberries are not.** Botanically, a berry is a fleshy fruit from a single flower with one ovary. Bananas qualify; strawberries develop from a flower with multiple ovaries, making them "accessory fruits" or "false fruits."
