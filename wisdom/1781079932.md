# Daily Facts

## The Shortest War in History
The Anglo-Zanzibar War of 1896 lasted between 38 and 45 minutes — making it the shortest war ever recorded. Zanzibar surrendered after British warships bombarded the sultan's palace. The conflict began at 9:02 AM and was over before most people had finished breakfast.

## Honey Never Expires
Archaeologists have found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that was still perfectly edible. Honey's low moisture content and natural acidity create an inhospitable environment for bacteria, making it essentially immortal food.
