# Peculiar Facts About Time

- A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus — it takes 243 Earth days to rotate once but only 225 Earth days to orbit the Sun.
- Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing (1969) than to the construction of the Great Pyramid (~2560 BCE).
- The shortest war in history lasted only 38-45 minutes: the Anglo-Zanzibar War of 1896.
