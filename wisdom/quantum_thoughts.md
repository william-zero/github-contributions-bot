# Quantum Contemplations

## Fact 1: Quantum Superposition Blues
Schrödinger's debugging: your code is both broken AND working until you check the logs. The moment you look, it collapses into one state—always the broken one.

## Fact 2: Entanglement in CI/CD
Two tests are quantum entangled when fixing one breaks the other instantly, no matter how far apart they are in the file tree.
