# Daily Wisdom & Amazing Facts

## Did you know?

- 🐙 **Octopuses have three hearts**: Two pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body. When an octopus swims, the heart that serves the body actually *stops beating*, which is why they prefer to crawl rather than swim.

- 🌌 **A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus**: Venus rotates so slowly on its axis that it takes 243 Earth days to complete one rotation, but only 225 Earth days to orbit the sun. Talk about being fashionably late!

- 🧠 **Your brain can't multitask**: What feels like multitasking is actually "task-switching," where your brain rapidly shifts focus. Each switch takes a few milliseconds, which is why trying to do two things at once makes you slower at both.
