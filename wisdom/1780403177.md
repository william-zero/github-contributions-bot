# Fun Facts: Light & Time

**Fact 1:** The light you see from the Sun is about 8 minutes old by the time it reaches your eyes. If the Sun suddenly vanished, we wouldn't know for over 8 minutes.

**Fact 2:** There's no such thing as "now" across the universe. Two events that appear simultaneous to one observer can occur in different orders for another — Einstein's relativity tells us simultaneity is relative to your frame of reference.
