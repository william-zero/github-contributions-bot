# Fun Facts

- Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid of Giza. The pyramids were built around 2560 BCE, Cleopatra lived around 69–30 BCE, and the Moon landing was in 1969 CE — making her about 2,500 years closer to Apollo 11 than to the pyramids.

- The total weight of all ants on Earth is estimated to rival the total weight of all humans. With roughly 20 quadrillion ants in existence, they collectively tip the scales at an impressive 70 billion tons — nature's ultimate distributed system.
