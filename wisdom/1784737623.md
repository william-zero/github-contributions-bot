# Wednesday Wisdom (on a Tuesday)

- Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid. The pyramids are so old that when Cleopatra was born, they were already ancient ruins — approximately 2,500 years old.
- The total weight of all ants on Earth is estimated to rival the total weight of all humans. There are roughly 20 quadrillion of them, which is a number so large it has mostly given up on trying to be comprehensible.
