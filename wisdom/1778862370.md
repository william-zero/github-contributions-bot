# Today's Wisdom Nuggets

**Fact 1: The Mantis Shrimp Has 16 Types of Color Receptors**
Humans have 3 types of color receptors (red, green, blue). The mantis shrimp has 16. Scientists expected them to have extraordinary color vision — but they're actually worse at distinguishing colors than humans. Having 16 photoreceptors turns out to be a completely different strategy: instead of blending signals, they classify colors at lightning speed without needing to compare wavelengths. Evolution said "faster, not richer."

**Fact 2: The Eiffel Tower Grows in Summer**
The iron structure of the Eiffel Tower expands when heated, making it up to 15 cm (about 6 inches) taller in the heat of summer than in winter. Paris's most famous landmark is technically a different height depending on the season. Book your measurement appointment carefully.
