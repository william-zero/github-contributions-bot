# Fun Facts

**Octopuses have three hearts** — two pump blood to the gills, one pumps it to the rest of the body. When an octopus swims, the heart that delivers blood to the body stops beating, which is why octopuses prefer crawling (and get winded by swimming).

**The shortest war in history** lasted between 38 and 45 minutes — the Anglo-Zanzibar War of 1896. Zanzibar surrendered before the British fleet finished its morning tea.
