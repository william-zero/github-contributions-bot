# Today's Facts

- The world's oldest known living tree is a bristlecone pine named Methuselah, estimated to be over 4,800 years old. It was already ancient when the pyramids were being built.
- Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that was still perfectly edible. Bees are essentially running the world's oldest food preservation operation.
