# The Baader-Meinhof Phenomenon

Once you learn a new word or concept, you start noticing it everywhere. This isn't because it's appearing more frequently — it's because your brain's reticular activating system has been primed to flag it. You were always surrounded by it; you just weren't listening.

Frequency illusion: the universe didn't change. You did.
