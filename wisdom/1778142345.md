# Today's Facts

**Mantis shrimp see 16 types of color receptors** — humans have 3, birds have 4, but mantis shrimp have 16, including UV and infrared. Yet studies suggest they're worse at distinguishing similar colors, using color like a fast barcode rather than a rich palette.

**A bolt of lightning is hotter than the sun's surface.** The surface of the sun sits around 5,500°C, while a lightning channel briefly reaches ~30,000°C — about 5x hotter — though it lasts only microseconds.
