# Weekend Curiosities

- Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid. The pyramids were built around 2560 BCE; Cleopatra was born ~69 BCE; the Moon landing was 1969 CE — a gap of about 2000 years on each side.
- Wombats are the only animals in the world that produce cube-shaped poop. The unique shape comes from their unusually long digestive tract stretching the stool into cubes.
- There are more possible iterations of a game of chess than there are atoms in the observable universe. After just four moves each, there are over 288 billion possible game positions.
