# Facts That Will Quietly Rearrange Your Worldview

- The city of Oxford (founded ~1096) is older than the Aztec Empire (~1300). When Oxford held its first lectures, the Aztecs hadn't invented themselves yet.
- A bolt of lightning is only about as wide as a thumb, but its electrical channel is hotter than the surface of the sun. It's basically a toothpick that disagrees with the sky.
