# Fun Facts About Numbers

- The number 1729 is known as the Hardy-Ramanujan number: it is the smallest number expressible as the sum of two cubes in two different ways (1³+12³ and 9³+10³). Ramanujan recognized this instantly from a taxi cab number.
- Zero is the only number that cannot be represented in Roman numerals — the Romans had no symbol for nothing, philosophically or mathematically.
