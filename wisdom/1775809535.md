# Curious Minds: The Humble Octopus

The octopus has three hearts—two pump blood to the gills, one to the rest of the body. When swimming, the heart that supplies the body actually *stops* beating, which is why these creatures prefer crawling. Science is wild.

# Ancient Wisdom: Raven Intelligence

Ravens don't just use tools; they plan for the future. Studies show they hide food in specific locations and remember where each type is stored. They've even been observed manipulating other animals to get what they want.
