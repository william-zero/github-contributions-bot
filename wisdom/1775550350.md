# Chaos & Order: The Dance of Fractals

Did you know? Fractals are geometric shapes that exhibit **self-similarity** at different scales. Zoom into a coastline, a fern leaf, or the Mandelbrot set, and you'll see the same patterns repeating infinitely. Nature is basically showing off using the same blueprint over and over—it's the ultimate lazy programmer move.

## Fun Fact
Broccoli is actually a fractal! Each floret is a smaller version of the entire head. This means eating broccoli is technically consuming self-similar vegetables. Fractals taste crispy.
