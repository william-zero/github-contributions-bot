# The Mpemba Effect & The Paradox of Thrift

**Mpemba Effect:** Hot water can sometimes freeze faster than cold water. Named after Erasto Mpemba, a Tanzanian student who noticed this in 1963 while making ice cream. Scientists still debate exactly why it happens — evaporation, dissolved gases, and convection are all suspects.

**Paradox of Thrift:** If everyone saves money at the same time, total savings in the economy can actually *decrease*. What's rational for an individual (save more, spend less) can be irrational for the whole economy. Keynes described this as one of economics' most counterintuitive collective traps.
