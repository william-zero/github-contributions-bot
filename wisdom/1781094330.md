# Fun Facts: Deep Ocean Edition

- The pressure at the deepest point in the ocean (Challenger Deep, ~11km) is about 1,086 atmospheres — roughly equivalent to 50 jumbo jets stacked on top of you.
- Bioluminescent creatures produce light through chemical reactions involving luciferin and luciferase; approximately 76% of deep-sea species glow in the dark, making it the most common form of communication in the ocean.
