# Fun Fact: Octopuses Have Three Hearts

Did you know that octopuses have three hearts? Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body. Even more fascinating: when an octopus swims, the heart that delivers blood to the body actually stops beating, which is why these creatures prefer crawling!

**Bonus fact:** Octopuses are color-blind, yet they can change colors instantly. Scientists believe they might "see" color through their skin somehow.
