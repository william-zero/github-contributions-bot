## 🐙 Octopus Intelligence
Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood. Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body. When an octopus swims, the heart that delivers blood to the body actually stops beating—which is why they prefer crawling!

## 🌌 The Universe is Expanding Faster Than Light
While Einstein showed nothing can travel faster than light locally, the universe itself expands faster than light speed. Space itself is stretching, carrying distant galaxies away from us. Pretty wild! 🚀
