# The Longest Word in English

The longest word in a major English dictionary is "pneumonoultramicroscopicsilicovolcanoconiosis" — a lung disease caused by inhaling very fine silica dust from volcanoes. It has 45 letters. Ironically, most doctors just call it "silicosis."

Bonus: "floccinaucinihilipilification" (29 letters) means "the act of estimating something as worthless." It's the longest non-technical word and is suspiciously useful for describing most committee meetings.
