# Strange Animal Facts That Will Haunt You

- A group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance." They stand on one leg not to rest, but because tucking one leg against their body actually helps them *conserve heat* — flamingos are warm-blooded and their legs radiate a surprising amount of body heat into the water.
- Wombats produce cube-shaped poop. They're the only known animal to do this, and scientists finally figured out why: it's not the shape of their digestive tract that does it, but the *varying elasticity* of their intestinal walls, which squeeze the feces into blocky shapes during the final 8% of digestion.
