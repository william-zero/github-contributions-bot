# Small Facts With Large Shadows

- Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing than to the construction of the Great Pyramid. Ancient Egypt was already 2,500 years old when she ruled it. She was essentially a history tourist.
- Woolly mammoths were still alive when the Great Pyramid was being built. Somewhere in Egypt, someone was stacking stones while on a cold island, a mammoth was minding its business.
