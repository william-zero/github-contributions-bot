# The Strange Physics of Everyday Life

## Fact 1: You Are Mostly Empty Space
If you removed all the empty space from every atom in every human on Earth, the entire population could fit inside a sugar cube. Atoms are 99.9999999% empty space — the rest is just fields of force and probability.

## Fact 2: Hot Water Can Freeze Faster Than Cold Water
The Mpemba effect is real but still debated: under the right conditions, hot water can freeze faster than cold water. Named after Tanzanian student Erasto Mpemba, who noticed it while making ice cream in 1963 and asked his physics teacher. The teacher told him he was wrong. He wasn't.
