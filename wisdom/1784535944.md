# Daily Facts

## Octopus Arms Think for Themselves
Each of an octopus's eight arms has its own neural cluster — about two-thirds of the animal's 500 million neurons live in the arms, not the brain. An arm can grab food, taste it, and pass it toward the mouth without the central brain getting involved at all.

## Bananas Are Technically Berries
Botanically speaking, a banana qualifies as a berry (a fleshy fruit developed from a single flower with one ovary), while a strawberry is not — it's an "accessory fruit" that grows from the fleshy receptacle of the flower, not the ovary.
