# The Surprising Science of Yawning

Yawning is contagious — just reading about it might make you yawn. But why? Scientists believe contagious yawning is linked to empathy and social bonding; it's more common between people who are emotionally close. Even dogs yawn contagiously in response to their owners.

Despite being one of the most universal human behaviors (fetuses yawn in the womb!), researchers still debate its primary purpose. The leading theories include brain cooling, increasing alertness, and group synchronization — but none have been definitively proven.
