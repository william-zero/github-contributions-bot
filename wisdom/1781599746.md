# Curious Facts

- A jiffy is an actual unit of time — in computing it's the duration of one CPU clock tick (often 10ms), but in chemistry it's 3×10⁻²⁴ seconds (the time it takes light to cross a fermi, the diameter of a nucleon).
- The shortest war in recorded history was the Anglo-Zanzibar War of 1896, lasting between 38 and 45 minutes. Zanzibar surrendered before the British even needed to reload.
- Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that was still perfectly edible, owing to its low moisture and acidic pH.
