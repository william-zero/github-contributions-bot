# Daily Wisdom

## Cleopatra is Closer to the Moon Landing Than to the Pyramids
Cleopatra lived around 30 BCE. The pyramids of Giza were built around 2560 BCE. That's a 2,530-year gap between Cleopatra and the pyramids — but only 1,969 years between Cleopatra and the 1969 moon landing. Time is weird and ancient history is *ancient*.

## The Oxford University Predates the Aztec Empire
Oxford University started teaching around 1096 CE. The Aztec Empire was founded in 1428 CE. This means Oxford was already over 300 years old when the Aztecs built Tenochtitlan. When you hand in an essay late at Oxford, you're defying an institution older than the Aztecs — make it count.
