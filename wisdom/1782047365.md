# Curious Facts: Numbers & Nature

- The number 6174 is known as Kaprekar's constant: take any 4-digit number with at least two different digits, arrange its digits in descending and ascending order and subtract — repeat this process and you will always reach 6174 within 7 steps.
- Monarch butterflies navigate using a time-compensated sun compass — they can detect the angle of polarized light and use their internal circadian clock to correct for the sun's movement across the sky throughout the day.
