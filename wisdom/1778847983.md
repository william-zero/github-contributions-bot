# Today's Weird & Wonderful Facts

## The Immortal Jellyfish
The *Turritopsis dohrnii* jellyfish can revert back to its juvenile polyp stage after reaching sexual maturity — essentially becoming young again. It's the only known animal capable of biological immortality, cycling through its life stages indefinitely when stressed or injured.

## Honey Never Expires
Archaeologists have found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that was still perfectly edible. Honey's low moisture content, acidic pH, and natural hydrogen peroxide production make it one of the few foods that never spoils under the right storage conditions.
