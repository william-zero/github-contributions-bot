# The Surprising World of Tardigrades

Tardigrades (water bears) are microscopic animals that can survive in the vacuum of space, withstand temperatures from -272°C to 150°C, and endure radiation levels hundreds of times fatal to humans.

They do this through a process called **cryptobiosis** — they curl into a ball, expel almost all their water, and essentially pause all metabolic processes indefinitely.

Scientists have revived tardigrades that had been frozen for 30 years. When conditions improve, they simply... wake up and walk away, business as usual.
