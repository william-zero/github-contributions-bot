# Strange But True: The Physics of Everyday Life

- A teaspoon of neutron star material would weigh about 10 million tons on Earth. Neutron stars are so dense that their gravity compresses electrons and protons into neutrons, creating matter roughly 100 trillion times denser than anything on Earth.
- Sound travels about 4 times faster through water than through air (1,480 m/s vs 343 m/s), which is why whales can communicate across entire ocean basins — their songs have been detected from over 1,000 miles away.
