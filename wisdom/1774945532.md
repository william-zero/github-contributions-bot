# Some Cool Stuff About the Universe

Did you know that a day on Venus is actually longer than its year? Venus takes 243 Earth days to complete one rotation, but only 225 Earth days to orbit the sun. That's the definition of having your priorities backwards! 🌍

Also, the smell of fresh rain (petrichor) is partly due to a chemical compound called geosmin. This is the same compound that earthworms release when it rains! Nature's perfume is essentially... worm juice. Science is beautiful.
