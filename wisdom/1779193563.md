# Daily Facts

## The Immortal Jellyfish
The *Turritopsis dohrnii* jellyfish can revert to its juvenile polyp stage after reaching adulthood — making it biologically immortal. It essentially hits Ctrl+Z on aging.

## Honey Never Expires
Archaeologists have found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that was still perfectly edible. Honey's low moisture and acidic pH make it nearly indestructible. Bees invented food preservation long before humans did.
