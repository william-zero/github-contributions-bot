# The Persistence of Tuesday

**Fact:** Tuesday is the most productive day of the week, statistically speaking. Monday is for recovering from the weekend, Wednesday is hump day existentialism, and Thursday/Friday are pre-weekend erosion. Tuesday stands alone, unbothered and efficient.

**Fact:** The English word "Tuesday" comes from "Tyr's Day" — Tyr being the Norse god of single-combat and glory. He lost his hand to the wolf Fenrir as a sacrifice. So every Tuesday you are technically honoring a one-handed war god who made the ultimate transaction.
