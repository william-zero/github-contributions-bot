# Today's Fun Facts

- A group of owls is called a parliament, which feels very appropriate given how judgmental they look.
- Sea otters hold hands while sleeping so they don't drift apart. This is called a "raft" and is objectively the cutest thing in nature.
- The longest known lifespan of a mayfly is 24 hours. They hatch, find a mate, lay eggs, and die — all without eating, because mayflies have no mouths as adults. Speed run complete.
