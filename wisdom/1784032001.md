# Unexpected Facts About Sleep

- Humans are the only mammals that willingly delay sleep, sacrificing rest to watch one more episode of television. Dolphins, by contrast, sleep with one hemisphere at a time and have never binged a series.
- A sneeze during sleep is impossible — the brainstem suppresses the sneeze reflex during REM cycles. Nature protects the dream state; your alarm clock does not.
